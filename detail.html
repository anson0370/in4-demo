What is Jambé and What Makes it Different

Jambé is a new electronic musical instrument that looks and feels like a traditional drum, but is so much more. Jambé is expressive, portable, and infinitely expandable. It allows anyone to create music and explore rhythm, using their hands, fingers, or drumsticks.

As you can tell by looking at Jambé, it was inspired by a drum. It is shaped and sized like a drum, and is built with all natural wood and metal materials. It even fits regular drum stands. Jambé feels comfortable in your hands or on your lap, and you can play it with regular drumsticks or with your hands, just like any traditional drum.

The Jambé playing surface is covered with a special combination of materials and sensors that read every nuance, from the full impact of a drumstick to the light tapping of a finger. Jambé transforms those gestures into expressive dynamic sounds. There are ten sensor zones which can be programmed as different sounds or effects, giving you a rich sound palette.

Jambé connects to an Apple iOS device, which provides an outstanding user interface. You can easily expand and upgrade your instrument with a few taps on the screen. Jambé comes with a wide variety of sounds, and our In-App store will carry additional sounds and kits, which will open up new playing possibilities.

An Entirely New Approach

With Jambé, we took an entirely new approach. We started by focusing on how to capture the natural movements and common gestures that go into playing. This unique data is sent to the iOS device, where our advanced algorithms and high-definition sounds accurately reproduce the performance.

The iOS device's high resolution touch screen makes it easy to configure Jambé without the need for tiny displays or small buttons found on other devices. You simply tap the kit you want and instantly your Jambé is now an entirely new instrument.

Who is the Jambé for

We designed Jambé to be responsive and robust enough for professional musicians, but affordable and accessible for students and music hobbyists. Jambé and an iOS device are all you need for a jam session or rehearsal.  It makes a great addition to a drum kit, or a complete solution by itself.
